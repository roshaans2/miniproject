A Brain Tumor disease diet is a healthy-eating plan that's naturally rich in nutrients and low in fat and calories. Key elements are fruits, vegetables and whole grains.


Recommended Foods


1. Broccoli
2. Yogurt
3. Tea
4. Dark, leafy greens

Foods to Avoid


There are several foods a person should try to limit when following the Brain Tumor diet. Foods containing the following can work against your goal of a Brain-healthy diet.


1. Unpasteurised milk or yoghurt made from unpasteurised milk
2. Certain soft, slow-ripened or blue-veined cheeses or cheeses made from unpasteurised milk (e.g. brie, camembert, Danish blue, stilton and gorgonzola).
3. Eggs (unless they are marked with a “Lion mark” and cooked until the white and yolk are solid)
4. Rare or undercooked meat and poultry

The Plate Method


One way to begin is to create a daily meal plan that emphasizes vegetables, fruits and whole grains and limits high-fat foods (such as red meat, cheese and baked goods) and high-sodium foods (such as canned or processed foods).
Follow these steps when preparing your plate:

  • Eggs.
  • Lean meat, chicken, and fish.
  • Beans, nuts, and seeds.
  • Cheese,nuts and seeds.
  • Add a serving of fruit or dairy and a drink of water or unsweetened tea or coffee.

A Sample Menu


When planning meals, take into account your size and activity level. The following menu is tailored for someone who needs 1,000 to 1,200 calories a day.

  • Breakfast - milk (1 cup) + soaked almond (8) + walnut (2-3) + idly(2).
  • Lunch - rice (300gms), khicadi(1-2 bowl), fish curry (1-2 pieces),water.
  • Snacks - Dark green leafy vegetables, fruit salad, milkshake.
  • Dinner - Salmon, porridge(1-2 bowls) , kadhi (1/2 bowl), milk.

What are the results of a Brain Tumor diet?


If you follow the diet eat more fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and low-fat dairy foods and less red meat, sweets, saturated fat, and sodium you may lower your blood pressure by 8 to 14 points.


It will also help you control your weight and may lower your risk of cancer.
