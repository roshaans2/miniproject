About Us

DOCINE is a disease prediction application which uses machine learning and deep learning. This application was developed by Roshaan, Roheth for our users. The main motive of this application is to predict whether you have the disease or not within few minutes from your home. This is the easiest way which gives us the maximum accuracy. Two methods are used here i.e. deep learning and machine learning. Diseases like Pneumonia, Malaria, Brain Tumor and COVID are predicted using deep learning whereas Diabetes, Heart diseases, Chronic Kidney diseases, Breast Cancer and Parkinson's disease are predicted using machine learning by the data provided from your side.
